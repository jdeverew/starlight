A compliance policy tells Intune what "healthy" looks like for a device. Combined with Conditional Access it gates access to corporate apps.

## Create a baseline policy

<Steps>

1. In the Intune admin center, go to **Devices → Compliance policies → Create policy**.
2. Choose **Windows 10 and later**.
3. Configure these baseline settings:
   - **BitLocker**: required
   - **Secure Boot**: required
   - **Minimum OS version**: set to the lowest build your org supports
   - **Antivirus**: required
4. Under **Actions for noncompliance**, mark the device noncompliant after one day.

</Steps>

## Assign and monitor

Assign the policy to a pilot group first. Monitor **Devices → Monitor → Noncompliant devices** for at least a week before expanding the scope.
